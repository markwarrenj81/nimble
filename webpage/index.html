    About Nimble

    XRPL Trade Bot

    A basic automated trading bot that is easy to use for opening buy and sale order in Binance. Just setup you API from your binance dashboard and let nimble do the rest in opening the orders based on "Mean reversion algorithm" to place your orders on binance. This bot was developed based on CryptoCowboy XRPL trading bot which is extended to work with Binance. Some explanation on how the orginal concept used in the orginal Bot by Michael Brown can be found here

    Use at your own risk

    Trading cryptocurrency is a very risky activity. Please make sure you have done your own research and use this tool at your own risk. This bot is intended as assist developers and investors to learn using a trading bot from the available APIs in this case, the Binance Exchange.

    Will extend more features for Nimble in the future as I get some feedbacks of its usage.
